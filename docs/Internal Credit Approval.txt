Internal Credit Approval & Loan Risk Assessment Policy
Document Code: CR-RISK-004
Version: 5.1
Effective Date: 15 March 2025
Department: Credit Risk & Corporate Banking
________________


1. Purpose
To define standardized procedures for:
* Retail loan approval

* SME credit evaluation

* Corporate loan risk assessment

* Collateral valuation

* Default management

________________


2. Loan Categories
2.1 Retail Loans
   * Personal loan

   * Auto loan

   * Mortgage

   * Education loan

2.2 SME Loans
      * Working capital

      * Equipment financing

      * Trade finance

2.3 Corporate Loans
         * Term loans

         * Syndicated loans

         * Revolving credit facilities

________________


3. Credit Scoring Model (Retail)
Score Components:
            * Income stability (30%)

            * Debt-to-income ratio (25%)

            * Credit history (20%)

            * Employment duration (15%)

            * Banking behavior (10%)

Minimum Approval Score: 65/100
Automatic rejection if:
               * DTI > 60%

               * Bankruptcy record within 5 years

               * Internal default history

________________


4. Collateral Requirements
Mortgage:
                  * Property valuation by approved appraiser

                  * Loan-to-value ratio ≤ 80%

Auto Loan:
                     * Vehicle age ≤ 5 years

                     * Mandatory insurance assignment to bank

SME Loan:
                        * Inventory pledge or personal guarantee required if risk score < 75

________________


5. Corporate Risk Assessment
Corporate risk rating based on:
                           * EBITDA stability

                           * Liquidity ratios

                           * Industry outlook

                           * Management track record

                           * Concentration risk

Rating Scale:
                              * A (Low Risk)

                              * B (Moderate)

                              * C (Elevated)

                              * D (High Risk)

                              * E (Unacceptable)

Loans rated D require Risk Committee approval.
Loans rated E must be rejected.
________________

7. Default & Recovery
Default defined as:
                                 * 90 days past due (retail)

                                 * 60 days past due (SME)

                                 * Covenant breach (corporate)

Recovery actions:
                                    * Restructuring

                                    * Collateral liquidation

                                    * Legal proceedings
